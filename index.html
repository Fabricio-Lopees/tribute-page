Steve Jobs Tribute Page

Timeline

February 24, 1955

Steven Paul Jobs is born in San Francisco.

1961

The Jobs family moves to Mountain View.

1968

Jobs calls Bill Hewlett, the co-founder and co-namesake of Hewlett-Packard.

1970

Meets future Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak through a friend.

1972

Graduates from Homestead High School in Cupertino.

1974

Begins a brief stint as an engineer at Atari.

1976

Co-founds Apple Computer with Wozniak and Roanld Wayne.

1977

Apple incorporates.

1980

Apple goes public, putting Jobs' net worth north of $200 million.

1984

Two days after the $1.5 million Ridley Scott-directed "1984" Super Bowl commercial airs.

1985

CEO John Sculley engineers Jobs' ouster from Apple.

1986

For $10 million, buys the Graphics Group division of Lucasfilm that becomes Pixar Animation Studios.

1988

NeXT Computer releases its first computer.

1993

NeXT discontinues hardware business, gets into software intead.

1995

Becomes Pixar's president and CEO.

1996

Returns to APple, as an adviser, after it buys NeXT for $429 million.

1997

Becomes CEO, initially as the de facto chief, then as interim chief in September.

1998

Apple returns to profitability and introduces the iMace, which becomes commercially available in August.

2000

Drops the "interim" from his CEO title at the Macworld Expo.

2001

Introduces iTunes, then exclusively for Mac users. Apple ships the first version of Mac OS X, code-named Cheetah and introduces the iPod.

2002

Apple introduces the first Windows-compatible iPods at Macworld.

2003

Opens the iTunes Music Store in the United States.

2004

Jobs undergoes surgery to remove a cancerous tumor in his pancreas.

2005

Apple introduces an iPod Shuffle.

2006

That year, Apple's market capitalization surpasses Dell's.

2007

Drops "Computer" from Apple's name. Introduces the iPhone.

2008

Introduces the App Store as an update to iTunes.

2009

Jobs takes a 6-month leave of absence for medical reasons.

2010

Introduces the iPad.

2011

Resigns from Apple CEO post; becomes chairman.
